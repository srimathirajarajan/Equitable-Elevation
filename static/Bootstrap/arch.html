SCHOOL OF ARCHITECTURE




Front view of Sri Manakula Vinayagar School of Architecture:-



Sri Manakula Vinayagar School of Architecture is the first institution in the Union Territory of Puducherry
to offer the five year full time B.Architecture course with an intake of 40 seats from the academic year 2017-18.

Career in Architecture is attractive, high paying and has lots of potential in terms of growth. Most of the jobs in
architecture are available in government organizations. Private construction companies require trained architects
who can carry out multi various construction projects for which they are willing to pay hefty salaries.

Ample job opportunities are available for architects having specialization in one segment or the other.
Numerous internationally acclaimed construction and real estate development companies provide employment
opportunities to the architects who are willing to explore career outside India.

Environment is conducive at Sri Manakula Vinayagar School of Architecture for learning and it welcomes all
the prospective students to join the School of Architecture considering the brightest opportunities.

The top architecture college in Pondicherry. B.Arch admission is open for Pondicherry, Tamilnadu and other state students.
The architecture schools course provides best training.
